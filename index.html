Frequently Asked Questions

Do we need to refactor our design system to use Françoise?
Not at all. Françoise offers Hybrid Support, meaning it works seamlessly with modern Variables, traditional Styles, and even legacy components simultaneously. You can start validating immediately without any prior restructuring.
Does Françoise train its AI on our design data?
Absolutely not. We guarantee contractually that your Intellectual Property is never used to train our models. We use Zero-Retention Processing, meaning your data is held in ephemeral memory only during the validation process and is immediately wiped afterward.
How does the Jira integration block tickets?
When a designer attempts to transition a Jira ticket to "Ready for Dev," our integration automatically checks the linked Figma frames. If Françoise detects critical errors (like WCAG 2.1 AA violations or broken token logic), the transition is blocked until the designer resolves the issues on the canvas.
Can we host Françoise on our own servers?
Yes, our Enterprise plan includes options for Private Cloud (VPC) or fully On-Premises deployment. This ensures that Françoise operates entirely within your organization's security perimeter, meeting the strict compliance needs of regulated industries.
