Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026 | Last updated: July 20, 2026

Françoise Design, Inc. ("Françoise", "we", "us") operates the Françoise Figma plugin and related website (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we handle and how we protect it. We designed Françoise around data minimization: we collect as little as possible and store almost nothing about your design work.

1. Who we are

Françoise Design, Inc., a Delaware corporation.
Registered address: 131 Continental Dr, Suite 305, Newark, Delaware 19713, USA.
Privacy contact: contact@francoise.design

2. What we collect — and what we don't

We deliberately keep collection minimal:

  • Figma user identifier (user_id): the only account identifier we store. We do not store your name or email in our database.
  • Design system data: stored in our database to power the Service.
  • Layout / mockup data: processed in memory only and never stored on our servers. Once processing completes, this data is gone.
  • Billing data: handled entirely by our payment provider (see Section 4). We do not receive or store card numbers.

We do not run analytics, marketing email, or tracking tools.

3. How the plugin accesses Figma

Françoise runs through the Figma Plugin API using the currentuser permission and network requests only. We access the design content you actively run the plugin on, and we do not retain your mockups after processing.

4. Service providers (subprocessors)

We share the limited data above only with providers strictly necessary to run the Service:

  • Microsoft Azure — hosting and database, region North Europe (EU).
  • Lemon Squeezy — Merchant of Record for payments; processes billing and card data on their systems.
  • Google (Gemini) — AI processing of design data. We are migrating this to Azure OpenAI. Your data is not used to train any AI models.

5. AI processing

Design data may be sent to our AI provider to perform Françoise's analysis and fixes. This data is used solely to deliver the result to you and is never used to train models. No personal data is required for this processing.

6. Data retention and deletion

Because layout data is never stored, there is little to retain. Stored data (design systems, Figma user_id) is deleted within 24 hours of your deletion request. Contact contact@francoise.design to request deletion.

7. International transfers

Data is hosted in the EU (Azure North Europe). We are based in Israel, which the EU recognizes as providing an adequate level of data protection. Where data is processed in the United States (e.g., our AI provider), we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses.

8. Security

We protect data with TLS encryption in transit, need-to-know access controls, two-factor authentication, and access logging. We are working toward SOC 2 compliance.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under the GDPR (access, correction, deletion, portability, objection) and the CCPA/CPRA (access, deletion, and the right to opt out of sale or sharing — note we do not sell or share personal information). To exercise any right, contact contact@francoise.design.

10. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for it to function. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

11. Business customers

Business customers can request a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) at contact@francoise.design.

12. Changes & Contact

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date reflects the current version. Material changes will be communicated where required.
Françoise Design, Inc. — contact@francoise.design

Security at Françoise

Françoise is built around a simple idea: the less data we hold, the safer your work is. We collect almost nothing and store even less. Here's exactly how we protect your design data.

Your mockups are never stored

When Françoise analyzes your file, layout and mockup data is processed in memory only and is never written to our servers. Once processing finishes, it's gone. There is nothing to leak, because we don't keep it.

We store the bare minimum

The only identifier we keep is your Figma user_id. We do not store your name or email address. Your design system data is stored solely to power the Service.

No AI training on your data

Françoise uses AI to analyze and fix your designs. Your data is used only to produce your result — it is never used to train any AI models.

EU hosting

Our infrastructure and database run on Microsoft Azure in the North Europe (EU) region. Data is encrypted in transit with TLS.

Minimal access

Access to customer data is restricted on a strict need-to-know basis — currently limited to our CTO. We use two-factor authentication and access logging. Everyone with access is bound by written confidentiality obligations.

Limited Figma permissions

The plugin runs through the Figma Plugin API using only the currentuser permission and network requests. We access only what you actively run the plugin on.

Fast deletion & Incident response

Because we store so little, cleanup is quick. On request, stored data is deleted within 24 hours. If a security incident affecting customer data ever occurs, we notify affected customers within 72 hours.

Trusted providers

We rely on a small set of vetted providers: Microsoft Azure (hosting), Lemon Squeezy (payments), and our AI provider (currently Google Gemini, migrating to Azure OpenAI). None of them use your data to train models.

On our roadmap & Working with us

We are actively working toward SOC 2 compliance. Business customers can request a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) and additional security documentation at contact@francoise.design.
