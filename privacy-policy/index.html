Privacy Policy

Welcome to Françoise ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (francoise.design) and use our automated design quality gate services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is strictly necessary to provide our services and communicate with you.

  • Information You Provide to Us: When you fill out our onboarding or demo request forms, we collect your Work Email, Role, Company Name, Team Size, and Design System Type.
  • Automatically Collected Information: We may collect standard basic log data (such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps) essential for maintaining website security and performance.

2. How We Handle Your Design Data

Security and IP protection are the core of our platform. When you use the Françoise Figma plugin:

  • Zero-Retention Processing: We process your design files and tokens in ephemeral memory. Once the validation process is complete, all analyzed design data is immediately and permanently wiped from our servers.
  • No AI Training: We guarantee that your Intellectual Property (IP), design systems, and usage patterns are never used to train our models or any third-party AI.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the contact information collected via our website forms exclusively to:

  • Schedule onboarding sessions or Enterprise demos.
  • Respond to your direct inquiries.
  • Send you critical technical or account updates regarding our service.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to facilitate our operations (e.g., routing demo requests via secure messaging protocols, or cloud hosting providers). These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and are permitted to use your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly.

6. Security

We implement robust, enterprise-grade technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to our leadership team:
