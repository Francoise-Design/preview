3. Purposes of Data Processing

We use your information to provide our services and improve our technology:

  • Service Delivery: Providing access to the Françoise AI platform and processing pilot program bookings.
  • AI Training Isolation: We process your design files solely to provide validation results. Your data is never used to train public models.
  • Support: Responding to technical inquiries and providing onboarding calibration.
  • Compliance: Fulfilling financial, tax, and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) requirements.

5. Data Retention

We store your data only as long as necessary:

  • Client Data: For the duration of the contract plus 5 years for tax compliance.
  • Design Assets: Automatically deleted 30 days after the end of the paid subscription or pilot program, or immediately upon request.
  • Marketing Data: Stored for 3 years after the last interaction or until you opt-out.

8. Data Processing for AI Products

Françoise AI uses a "Privacy-First" architecture. Unlike consumer AI tools, our Enterprise Gate ensures:

  • No Public Training: Data uploaded to our Figma plugin is isolated to your organization's environment.
  • Secure API: We use Enterprise-tier APIs from providers (like Google Gemini or OpenAI) that contractually prohibit the use of customer data for model training.
  • Actionable Privacy: All visual processing is done in real-time, and files are not stored longer than required for the validation session.

11. Your Rights

  • Request rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.
  • Data portability in machine-readable formats (JSON/CSV).
