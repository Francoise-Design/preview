Integration & Security

Hybrid Architecture

Enterprise-grade security. Your design data never trains public models. We use a mix of strict algorithms and targeted AI.

Algorithmic Core

90% of validation (Token structure, Nesting, WCAG math) is deterministic. Fast, predictable, zero external processing.

Targeted AI Nodes

LLMs/CV are triggered only for specific nodes (e.g. "Card vs Modal"). We feed isolated fragments, not full files.

Pre-Trained Models

We do not need your data to train models, and we do not retrain on client inputs.

Zero Retention

All data is ephemeral. Processed in memory and deleted immediately after the report.
